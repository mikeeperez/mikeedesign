About Mike E. Perez

Mike E. Perez is a multidisciplinary designer creating innovative solutions and stunning graphics for brands across the world. A true digital nomad, he currently splits his time between Tokyo and Texas.

For nearly 20 years, Mike has brought his expertise in graphic design, art direction, typography, photography, and brand development to companies across a variety of industries. He has a bachelor of fine arts in communication design from Texas State University and, in 2018, he completed his digital marketing certification at Southern Methodist University.

He is a devoted husband, father, and self-proclaimed geek—at one time having the world’s most popular Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles fan site. He enjoys exploring Tokyo (and the rest of the world) with his husband Brad and daughter Amalia.
